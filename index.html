Genaille–Lucas Rulers Visualiser

Genaille–Lucas Rulers Visualiser by James Harris

This tool is a visulaiser made for Genaille–Lucas Rulers. For more information about these rulers see their wikipedia page

How to use: enter a number upto 15 digits into the text entry field and select a single non zero digit from

the drop down. Press solve to instantly display the answer on the rulers. Alternativley you can repeatedly click step solve to step through the solution.

The last option is to click slow solve, which will step through the solution for you.
